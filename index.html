Chocolate Cake

30 Minutes
Serves 4
450 Calories

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Instructions

  1. Mix all dry ingredients.
  2. Add eggs and milk.
  3. Whisk until smooth.
  4. Pour batter into tray.
  5. Bake at 180°C for 30 minutes.
